Club Pulse Ghana

Ghana — For the love of the game

Site Purpose

Club Pulse Ghana provides a centralized hub for Ghanaian football fans. The site hosts club profiles, match events, photo galleries, a fan signup form, and news so fans, players, and local clubs can connect and share information.

  • Club profiles and badge gallery
  • Match calendar and upcoming events
  • Fan community sign-up and recent signups
  • Photo gallery and featured stadium/park images

Visitor Scenarios

  1. When is the next Asante Kotoko match and where will it be played? — Answer: Events page with date, venue, and ticket info.
  2. How do I join the fan club and receive match updates? — Answer: Community page with signup form and newsletter opt-in.
